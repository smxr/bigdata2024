http://bigdataieee.org/BigData2024/
IEEE BigData 2024- Washington DC, USA , December 15-18, 2024

The IEEE Big Data 2024 has received more than 750 full papers in the main conference and industry and government program. If you miss the submission deadline, there are still chances for you to submit your research work to the IEEE Big Data 2024 workshops and Posters. The workshop papers and posters are published in the conference proceedings. (EI indexed)

(2) Poster (Submission deadline: Nov 15, 2024)

Poster abstracts are limited to one page, the format and style are flexible. Posters will be reviewed on the basis of their relevance to the scope of the conference and on the scientific quality of the work presented. Each poster paper will be published with maximum up to 3 page limit in the poster/workshop proceedings (EI indexed). (format instruction for publication will be included with the poster acceptance notification)
