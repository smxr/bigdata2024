IEEE Big Data 2024 Call for Sponsorship

December 15-18, 2024 - Washington DC, USA
2024 IEEE International Conference on Big Data (IEEE BigData 2024)

The IEEE International Conference on Big Data 2024 (IEEE BigData 2024) provides a leading forum for disseminating the latest research in Big Data. IEEE Big Data brings together leading researchers and developers from academia, research and the industry from all over the world to facilitate innovation, knowledge transfer and technical progress in addressing the 5 V’s (Velocity, Volume, Variety, Value and Veracity) of Big Data. The purpose of the conference is to identify deep technical and scientific nature of big data problems, and share the future direction on the development of next-generation solutions for data-driven decision making. The conference will attract high-quality theory and applied research findings in big data science and foundations, big data infrastructure, big data management, big data search & mining, privacy/security, and big data applications. The attendees will get the opportunity to learn about the recent research results, and be able to network with some of the leading luminaries, researchers and practitioners in this area. It is a unique place to identify deep technical and scientific nature of big data problems, share the direction on the development of next-generation solutions for data-driven decision making, and shape the future of Big Data technology.

Here is the summary of the conferences in the previous years:
The IEEE Big Data conference series started in 2013 has established itself as the top tier research conference in Big Data.
  1. The IEEE Big Data 2023 (http://bigdataieee.org/BigData2023/ , regular paper acceptance rate: 17.4%) was held in Sorrento, Italy, Dec 15-18, 2023 with close to 950 registered participants from 50 countries.
  2. The IEEE Big Data 2022 ( http://bigdataieee.org/BigData2022/ , regular paper acceptance rate: 19.2%) was held in Osaka, Japan, Dec 17-20, 2022 with close to 1250 registered participants from 54 countries.
  3. The first conference IEEE Big Data 2013 ( http://bigdataieee.org/BigData2013/ regular paper acceptance rate: 17.0%) was held in Santa Clara, CA from Oct 6-9, 2013 with more than 400 registered participants from 40 countries.

Why Sponsor and Exhibit?

* Stand out in the crowd
Over the past few years, IEEE BigData Conference has brought together thousands of representatives from diverse government agencies, industrial companies, universities, and research organizations from more than 50 countries around the world. IEEE BigData Conference is the best place to show yourselves in fast-growing Big Data research and application fields, which can inform and inspire your product development, transform the results of company's research into the newest products, best services, and great sales.

* Diverse Programs and Attendees
IEEE BigData conference attracts researchers, scientists, software developers, system engineers, decision makers, corporate managers, CIOs/CTOs, etc. all over the world. The diverse programs will give attendees the opportunity to learn latest technologies and new trends, explore the new products and solutions they need to stay at the leading edge of their professions.

* Levels of sponsorship or Exhibiting
IEEE BigData conference offers organizations maximum exposure and recognition with this event. Take advantage of these great promotional opportunities and select the level of sponsorship or exhibiting best suited for your organization to enhance your visibility, draw attention to your products/services, and increase booth traffic. The sponsor levels are Platinum Sponsor, Gold Sponsor, Silver Sponsor, Bronze Sponsor, and Exhibit Sponsor.

Sponsorship Opportunities

IEEE Big Data 2024 offers a variety of sponsorship opportunities suitable for large as well as small organizations. The sponsors get visibility for their companies, demonstrate their support of big data related research and development, and contribute to the success of the conference. Four levels of sponsorships are available.
  • Platinum $20,000
  • Gold $15,000
  • Silver $12,000
  • Bronze $6,000
  • Exhibit $2,500

In-kind sponsorships that directly defray the costs of the conference are also welcome, (for example, sponsorship for a student travel scholarship), in these cases, the sponsor name is prominently displayed.

Sponsorship Benefits

The sponsor of IEEE BIG Data Conference at any level will be listed on the IEEE BigData 2024 homepage, program guide, and conference proceedings and will be acknowledged during remarks.

All sponsors will be offered one or more tabletop spaces to exhibit your products or services as well as interact with conference attendees. This is a great opportunity to network and will be located near the main sessions and breaks.

All sponsors will be offered “Job Fair” posters to post jobs and internships in the conference.
